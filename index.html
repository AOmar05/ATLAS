What We Do

Expert care for
every garment.

From everyday laundry to delicate dry cleaning and skilled tailoring — all under one roof on Wilmslow Road.

Dry Cleaning

Professional solvent-based cleaning for suits, dresses, coats and delicates. Gentle on fabrics, tough on stains.

Laundry Service

Wash, dry and fold service for everyday items, uniforms, bedding and workwear. Ready in as little as 24 hours.

Tailoring & Alterations

Skilled in-house alterations — hemming, resizing, repairs and bespoke adjustments. We make clothes fit perfectly.

Uniform Cleaning

Regular, reliable uniform and workwear cleaning for businesses. Consistent quality, flexible scheduling.

Household Items

Curtains, duvets, cushion covers and more. We handle large household items that most cleaners turn away.

Express Service

Need it fast? Our same-day express service gets your items back the same day when dropped off before 10am.
